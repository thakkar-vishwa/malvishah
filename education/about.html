advanced & Hi-Tech Labs All the engineering departments are well equipped with modern laboratories to cater to the need of training, the students as well as for industrial testing. Ragging Free Campus Anti-Ragging Squad monitors, directs and oversees the functions and performance of the institute to prevent the ragging in the institution and also to safeguard the rights of female students,faculties and staff members. Well-Experienced Faculties The institute has an extremely well qualified and experienced faculty that make the students excel in every possible scholastic and co-scholastic field. Smart Class Smart technology-enhanced classrooms that foster opportunities for teaching and learning by integrating cutting-edge technologies. Students of the college have been continuously bringing pride for institute by getting ranks in their participation in national and international events of sports as well as cultural events.
